1. PROJECTION SHADOWS:
    Advantages: simple to implement, without significant impact on performance.
    Disadvantages: projection shadows may lack detail when dealing with complex geometries. They may also struggle when objects cast shadows on themselves.

  2. SHADOW MAPPING:
    Advantages: very precise and detailed, can handle self-shadowing. Can be adapted to various scenes and lighting conditions.
    Disadvantages: can suffer from aliasing, requires the extra computational step of calculating the shadow map.
